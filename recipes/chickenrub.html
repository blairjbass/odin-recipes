Chicken rub

Ingredients

Note: Each 'part' equals 1 tsp for every 4 lbs of chicken
  • 1 part salt
  • 2 parts pepper
  • 2 parts garlic powder
  • 1 part sugar
  • 2 parts paprika
  1. Mix the spices until well blended in a bowl.
  2. Rub some oil over chicekn. Rub spices on oiled chicken.
  3. Let sit in refrigerator for 4-24 hours.
