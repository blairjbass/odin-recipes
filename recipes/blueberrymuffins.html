Blueberry muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1.5 cups blueberries (tossed in flour)
  • 2 tbsp sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt together
  3. In a different bowl, mix oil and sugar well, then add and beat egg then add vanilla
  4. Gradually add flour mixture and milk (alternate)
  5. Add blueberries, mix
  6. Scoop batter into muffin tin. Sprinkle 2 tbsp sugar over top
  7. Bake 30 minutes and let stand 10 minutes
