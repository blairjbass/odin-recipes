Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 small banana, mashed
  • 1 small egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Beat the egg then add in banana, milk, oil, vanilla. Mix well
  2. In a separate bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon.
  3. Gradually add flour mixture to first bowl. Mix well.
  4. Melt butter over medium heat. Add batter. Cook until done (usually about 2-3 minutes per side).
